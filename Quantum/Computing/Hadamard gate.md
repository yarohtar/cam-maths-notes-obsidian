[[Logic Gates]]
$$
H=\frac{1}{\sqrt{ 2 }}\begin{pmatrix}
1 & 1 \\
1 & -1
\end{pmatrix}
$$
or in [[Dirac Notation]] 
$$
H=\frac{1}{\sqrt{ 2 }}(\ket{0} \bra{0} +\ket{0} \bra{1} +\ket{1} \bra{0} -\ket{1} \bra{1} )
$$
We can check:
- $H\ket{0}=\ket{+}$
- $H\ket{1}=\ket{-}$
- $H^{\dagger}=H$ so [[Hermetian]]
- $H^{2}=I$
- $H\ket{+}=\ket{0}$
- $H\ket{-}=\ket{1}$
- $H=F_{2}$ for [[Quantum Fourier Transform]]
