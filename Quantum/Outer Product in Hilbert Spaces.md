$\ket{a}\bra{b}\in M_{n}(\mathbb{C})$
$$
\ket{a} \bra{b} =\begin{pmatrix}
a_{1} \\
\vdots \\
a_{n}
\end{pmatrix}
\begin{pmatrix}
b_{1}^{*} & \dots & b_{n}^{*}
\end{pmatrix}
=
\begin{pmatrix}
a_{1}b_{1}^{*} & \dots & a_{1}b_{n}^{*} \\
\vdots &  & \vdots \\
a_{n}b_{1}^{*} & \dots & a_{n}b_{n}^{*}
\end{pmatrix}$$
Note that $I=\sum_{i=1}^{n}\ket{e_{i}}\bra{e_{i}}$ where $\{ e_{i} \}$ is an orthonormal basis.
