# Homothetic Copy

Let $S \subset \mathbb{N}^d$ be a finite set. A homothetic copy of $S$ is any set of the form $a + \lambda S$ where $a \in \mathbb{N}^d$ and $\lambda \in \mathbb{N}$. For example, in $\mathbb{N}^1$, a homothetic copy of $\{1, 2, \dots, m\}$ is precisely an arithmetic progression of length $m$.
