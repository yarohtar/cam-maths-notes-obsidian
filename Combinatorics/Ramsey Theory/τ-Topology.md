The usual topology or τ-topology on $[\mathbb{N}]^\omega$ has basic open sets $\{ L : A \subseteq L \}$ for finite $A \subseteq \mathbb{N}$.

The metric is $d(L, M) = 0$ if $L = M$, else $1/\min(L \Delta M)$.

This is the subspace topology from the [[Product Topology|product topology]] on $\{0,1\}^\mathbb{N}$.
