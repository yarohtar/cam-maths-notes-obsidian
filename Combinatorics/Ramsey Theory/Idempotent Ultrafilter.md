An idempotent ultrafilter $u$ on $\mathbb{N}$ satisfies $u + u = u$, where $+$ is the operation defined by $u + v = \{ A : \forall x \in u \forall y \in v \, x + y \in A \}$.

## Idempotent Lemma

If $X$ is a [[Compact Hausdorff is Normal|compact Hausdorff]] space with an associative left-continuous binary operation, then there exists an idempotent element $x$ with $x + x = x$.

## Proof

Consider the set of compact subsets $Y \subseteq X$ with $Y + Y \subseteq Y$.

This has a minimal element by [[Zorn's Lemma]], and then construct the idempotent.

## Corollary

There exists an idempotent ultrafilter on $\mathbb{N}$.
