Let $A$ be a rational matrix with columns $c_1, \dots, c_n$. $A$ has the columns property if there exists a partition $B_1 \cup \dots \cup B_r$ of $\{1, \dots, n\}$ such that $\sum_{i\in B_1} c_i = 0$, and for $s\geq 2$, $\sum_{i\in B_s} c_i \in \operatorname{span}\{ c_j : j \in \bigcup_{t=1}^{s-1} B_t \}$. Then $A$ is called having the columns property.
