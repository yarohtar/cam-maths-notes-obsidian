Consider a [[Stationary Source]] $X_{1},X_{2},\dots$ on a finite alphabet $A$.
Let $T:A^{\mathbb{Z}}\to A^{\mathbb{Z}}$ be the shift operator $(Tx)_{n}=x_{n+1}$.
The source $X$ is ergodic if all $T$-invariant measurable sets are trivial i.e. 
$$
TB=B \implies \mathbb{P}(X_{-\infty}^{\infty}\in B)\in \{ 0,1 \}
$$
