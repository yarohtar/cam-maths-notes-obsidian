Consider $\mathcal{H}_{AB}=\mathcal{H}_{A}\otimes \mathcal{H}_{B}$.
Let $\ket{a_{i}}$ be a basis for $\mathcal{H}_{A}$ and $\ket{b_{i}}$ be a basis for $\mathcal{H}_{B}$.
Then define $\operatorname{Tr}_{B} : \mathcal{B}(\mathcal{H}_{AB}) \to \mathcal{B}(\mathcal{H}_{A})$ by:
$$
\operatorname{Tr}_{B}(\ket{a_{i}} \bra{a_{j}} \otimes \ket{b_{k}} \bra{b_{l}} ) = \operatorname{Tr}(\ket{b_{k}}\bra{b_{l}} ) \ket{a_{i}} \bra{a_{j}} 
$$
where $\operatorname{Tr}$ is the [[Trace]].
Note that this extends to all [[Linear Operator]]s in $\mathcal{B}(\mathcal{H}_{AB})$ by linearity.
