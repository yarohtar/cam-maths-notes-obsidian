### Definition (path)
Let $X$ be a space with $x_{0},x_{1}\in X$. A path from $x_{0}$ to $x_{1}$ is a map $\gamma:I\to X$ st $\gamma(0)=x_{0}$ and $\gamma(1)=x_{1}$.
If $x_{0}=x_{1}$ then $\gamma$ is a loop based at $x_{0}$.
#### Notation
$\gamma:x_{0}\leadsto x_{1}$
