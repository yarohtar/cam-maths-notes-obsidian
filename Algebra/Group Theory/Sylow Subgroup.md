Let $p$ be prime and $G$ a finite [[Group]] of order $p^am$ where $p \nmid m$. 
A Sylow $p$-subgroup of $G$ is $P\leq G$ s.t. $|P|=p^a$. 
[[Groups of prime order]]
The set of Sylow $p$-subgroups is denoted by $Syl_p(G)=\{P\leq G: |P|=p^a\}$.
