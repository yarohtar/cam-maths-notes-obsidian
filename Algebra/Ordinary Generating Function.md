Given the [[Unlabelled Structure]] $\mathcal{A}:\mathcal{B}\to \mathcal{B}_{R}$
its ordinary generating function as a [[Formal Power Series]] in $R[[x]]$ is:
$$
f_{\mathcal{A}}(x) = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty}\sum_{\alpha\in A_{n}} w(\alpha) x^{n}
$$
where $A_{n}=\mathcal{A}[n]$
