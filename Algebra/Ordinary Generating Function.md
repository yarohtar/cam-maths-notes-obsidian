Given the [[Unlabelled Structure]] from $F:\mathcal{B}\to \mathcal{B}_{R}$
its ordinary generating function as a [[Formal Power Series]] in $R[[x]]$ is:
$$
f_{F}(x) = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty}\sum_{\alpha\in F_{n}} w(\alpha) x^{n}
$$
where $F_{n}$ is the set of equivalence classes $F[n]/\sim$
