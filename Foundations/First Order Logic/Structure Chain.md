Let $\alpha$ be a limit [[Ordinal]].
A collection $(\mathcal{M}_{i})_{i<\alpha}$ of $\mathcal{L}$-[[Structure]]s is a chain if 
for all $i<j<\alpha$:
$$
\mathcal{M}_{i}\subseteq \mathcal{M}_{j}
$$
### Lemma
Let 
$$
\mathcal{M}=\bigcup_{i<\alpha} M_{i}
$$
Then $\mathcal{M}$ is a well defined [[Structure]].
