Let $P$ be a [[Poset]].
We say that $A\subseteq P$ is a strong antichain 
if any two distinct elements of $A$ are [[Incompatible]]:
$$
(\forall a,b\in A)\ (\exists r\leq a,b) \implies a=b
$$
Note that his is a stronger notion than that of [[Antichain]].
(and is the one used in [[Forcing and Continuum Hypothesis]]).
When the context is clear, we will just say antichain.
