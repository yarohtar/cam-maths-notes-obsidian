Let $B$ be a [[Boolean Algebra]] and $\mathcal{U}$ an [[Ultrafilter]] on $B$.
In particular:
$$
\begin{gather}
a\in \mathcal{U}\land a\leq b\implies b\in \mathcal{U} \\
a\in \mathcal{U} \land b\in \mathcal{U} \implies a\land b\in \mathcal{U} \\
a \in \mathcal{U} \iff \neg a\not\in \mathcal{U}
\end{gather}
$$
(the last property is easy but not trivial)

We define a quantifier meaning "for $\mathcal{U}$-most $x$" as follows:
$$
\forall_{\mathcal{U}}x\ \phi(x) \iff \{ j\in J: \phi(j) \} \in \mathcal{U}
$$
