Let $J$ be a set.
Then there is an [[Ultrafilter]] $\mathcal{U}$ on $\mathcal{P}(J)$ ordered by $\subseteq$.
In particular, this is the [[Boolean Algebra Ultrafilter]] on $\mathcal{P}(J)$.
We define a quantifier meaning "for $\mathcal{U}$-most $x$":
$$
\forall_{\mathcal{U}}x\ \phi(x) \iff \{ j\in J:\phi(j) \} \in \mathcal{U}
$$
