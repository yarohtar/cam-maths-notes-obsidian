Let $(\mathbb{P},\leq,\mathbb{1})$ be a [[Forcing Partial Order]].
Let $F\subseteq \mathbb{P}$ be a [[Filter Base]].
Suppose that for any $p,q\in \mathbb{P}$: 
$$
p\leq q \land p\in F \implies q\in F
$$ 
Then $F$ is a filter.
### Lemma
Let $B$ be a [[Filter Base]].
Then 
$$
\{ p\in \mathbb{P}: (\exists q\in B)\,q\leq p \}
$$
is a [[Filter]] - the filter generated by $B$.
