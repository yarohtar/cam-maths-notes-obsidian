Let $\mathcal{M}$ be an $\mathcal{L}$-[[Structure]]. 
The theory of $\mathcal{M}$ is 
$$
\mathrm{Th}_{\mathcal{L}}(\mathcal{M}) = \{ \varphi:\varphi \text{ is an }\mathcal{L}\text{-sentence and }M\models \varphi \}
$$
We write $\mathrm{Th}(\mathcal{M})$ when $\mathcal{L}$ is clear.
Note that $\mathrm{Th}(\mathcal{M})$ is always a [[Complete Theory]]
