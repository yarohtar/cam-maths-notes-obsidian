Similar to [[TST matrix]], matrices
$$
S=\begin{pmatrix}
\alpha & \beta &  \\
-\beta & \alpha & \ddots &  \\
 & \ddots & \ddots & \beta \\
 &  & -\beta & \alpha
\end{pmatrix}
$$
Have eigenvalues:
$$
\lambda_{k} = \alpha+2i\beta \cos \frac{k\pi}{M+1}
$$
with eigenvectors:
$$
(w_{k})_{m} = i^{m}\sin \frac{km\pi}{M+1}
$$
