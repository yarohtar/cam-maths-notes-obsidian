[[Laplace Method]]
$$
\int_{0}^{\infty} u^{n} e^{-Au^{p}} du = \frac{A^{-(n+1)/p}}{p} \Gamma\left( \frac{n+1}{p} \right)
$$
$$
\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} u^{2m} e^{-Au^{2q}} du = \frac{A^{-(2m+1)/(2q)}}{q} \Gamma\left( \frac{2m+1}{2q} \right)
$$
$$
\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} u^{2m+1} e^{-Au^{2q}}du = 0
$$
