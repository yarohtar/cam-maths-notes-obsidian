Then Alice cannot convey any info to Bob just by doing local measurement on $A$.

$$
\ket{\phi_{AB}} =\sum c_{ab}\ket{a} \ket{b}
$$
Then 
$$

p(b)&=\bra{\phi_{AB}} I_{A}\otimes \ket{b} \bra{b} \ket{\phi_{AB}} \\
