Act on $\mathcal{H}=\mathbb{C}^{2}$
$$
\begin{gather}
X=\sigma_{x}=\begin{pmatrix}
0 & 1 \\
1 & 0
\end{pmatrix}\\
Y=\sigma_{y}=\begin{pmatrix}
0 & -i \\
i & 0
\end{pmatrix}\\
Z=\sigma_{z}=\begin{pmatrix}
1 & 0 \\
0 & -1
\end{pmatrix}

\end{gather}
$$
#### Properties
- They are [[Hermetian operator]] and unitary
- They anticommute $\{ X,Y \}=0$
